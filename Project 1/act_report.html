After analyzing the dog names in the dataset, we found that the most common dog name is Charlie. This insight is visually represented in a bar chart, showcasing the top 10 most common dog names and their frequencies.

Insight 2: Relationship between Favorited Tweets and Retweet Counts

By examining the relationship between favorite counts and retweet counts, we discovered a positive correlation. This insight is effectively communicated through a scatter plot, which displays the distribution of favorited tweets and retweet counts.

Visualization: Engagement level of tweets over time

To analyze the engagement level of tweets, we plotted the number of retweets and favorites (likes) over time using a line chart. This visualization helps identify any patterns or trends in tweet engagement.

Through the analysis of WeRateDogs Twitter data, we have gained valuable insights into dog names, engagement level, and dog ratings.
